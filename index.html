Crumpet Animal Shelter

Four legs or two, we shelter you!

Contact us, or come visit!

In town and looking for a rewarding afternoon? Come down to the shelter and volunteer to help our buddies by feeding, cleaning and playing with them, alongside loads of other jobs. Our guests may not be abe to say thank you, but they appreciate your time and affection more than anything!
